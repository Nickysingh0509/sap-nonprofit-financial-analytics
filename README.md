# 📊 SAP Nonprofit Financial Analytics Project

## **Executive KPI Dashboard Suite – SAP Analytics Cloud**

---

## **🎯 Project Overview**

This project was developed using **SAP Analytics Cloud (SAP UCC University Environment)** to evaluate the financial health, fundraising sustainability, and donor engagement performance of a nonprofit organization.

The objective of this dashboard suite was to:

- Monitor financial stability through key ratios  
- Analyze fundraising source performance  
- Evaluate budget vs actual performance  
- Assess income vs expense sustainability  
- Track contributor growth and retention  
- Identify donation behavior trends  

The dashboards were built to support **executive-level financial decision-making**.

---

## **🧾 Business Context**

Nonprofit organizations rely heavily on sustainable fundraising strategies, donor retention, and disciplined financial planning.

This project evaluates:

- Reserve strength
- Revenue diversification
- Donor engagement trends
- Operational surplus/deficit conditions
- Budget performance gaps

The analysis focuses on multi-year trends (2007–2011) to identify structural financial patterns.

---

## **📌 Key Analytical Modules**

---

## **1️⃣ Operating Reserve Ratio**

### **Purpose**
Measures financial stability using unrestricted net assets relative to operating expenses.

### **Insight**
- The reserve ratio falls in the **Warning Zone (10–24.99%)**.
- Reserves are below the ideal threshold of **25%+**.
- Moderate liquidity exists but financial flexibility is limited.

### **Business Implication**
Lower reserve levels increase vulnerability to unexpected expenses or revenue fluctuations.

### **Recommendation**
Focus on strengthening unrestricted reserves to improve long-term sustainability.

---

## **2️⃣ Fundraising Mix Analysis (2007–2011)**

### **Purpose**
Evaluates contribution distribution across:

- Corporate
- Individual
- Foundations
- Special Events

### **Insight**
- Corporate and Special Events contribute the largest revenue share.
- Foundation support remains smaller in proportion.
- Year-over-year fluctuations are visible but corporate remains dominant.

### **Business Implication**
Revenue concentration increases strategic dependency on key fundraising channels.

### **Recommendation**
Diversify funding sources while strengthening high-performing corporate partnerships.

---

## **3️⃣ Performance to Budget (FY2011)**

### **Purpose**
Compares actual fundraising performance against planned budget targets.

### **Insight**
- Individual and foundation donations exceeded budget.
- Corporate and special events underperformed relative to target.
- Overall budget gaps indicate operational funding risk.

### **Business Implication**
Revenue forecasting assumptions may need recalibration.

### **Recommendation**
Enhance sponsorship strategy and optimize event execution to close budget gaps.

---

## **4️⃣ Income vs Expenses Overview (2010–2011)**

### **Purpose**
Determines surplus or deficit position across fiscal years.

### **Insight**
- 2011 shows operational surplus.
- 2010 reflected a deficit condition.
- Improved revenue discipline contributed to recovery.

### **Business Implication**
Cost management and revenue growth strategy positively impacted financial stability.

### **Recommendation**
Maintain cost controls and protect revenue growth channels.

---

## **5️⃣ Contributor Trend Analysis**

### **Purpose**
Analyzes year-over-year contributor growth and donation movement.

### **Insight**
- Majority of contributors showed upward or stable donation trends.
- Contributor engagement appears generally healthy.
- Participation levels remain stable even when average donation fluctuates.

### **Business Implication**
Strong donor relationships support long-term sustainability.

### **Recommendation**
Prioritize relationship management for upward-trending contributors.

---

## **6️⃣ Repeat vs New Contributor Analysis**

### **Purpose**
Evaluates donor retention rate.

### **Insight**
- Percentage of repeat contributors falls below 50% (Critical Zone).
- Heavy reliance on new donors.
- Low retention weakens long-term revenue stability.

### **Business Implication**
Acquisition-focused fundraising increases cost and reduces predictability.

### **Recommendation**
Implement structured donor retention strategies:
- Follow-up engagement campaigns
- Recognition programs
- Long-term donor nurturing initiatives

---

## **7️⃣ Average Gift Size Trend (2007–2011)**

### **Purpose**
Measures volatility in donation size.

### **Insight**
- Significant dip in 2008.
- Recovery and peak performance in 2011.
- Donation behavior influenced by external economic conditions.

### **Business Implication**
Gift volatility impacts forecasting reliability.

### **Recommendation**
Stabilize donor confidence through strategic communication and engagement consistency.
